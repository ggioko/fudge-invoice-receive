FAQS

How can I upload an Invoice?

You can log in to your account and submit an xml file, or upload via email

How do I send to another account?

You can log in to your account and enter another user's username to send them an invoice

How do upload via email?

Send an email containing a raw invoice xml file/s to invoicerecvapi@gmail.com. You can cc or address the email to other recipients and they will also receive the invoice on our platform

Do I need an account to upload via email?

You don't need an account to upload to our platform via email. We will save your invoice and email along with it so it is accessible to you, or any other recipients, if you join our platform in the future.

What invoices will be accepted?

Invoices that comply with Australian ubl invoice standards will be accepted. For more information see link text

How can I get in touch with Invoice Platform?

Send us an email at invoicerecvapi@gmail.com
